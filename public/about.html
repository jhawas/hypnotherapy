JOur Code of Ethics and Standards

Hypnotherapists holding a certification from the National Guild of Hypnotists regard the welfare of their client as their primary obligation. They operate on principles of equality, professionalism and non-discrimination. All information received is treated in the strictest confidence and without judgement. Respect is given to a client’s needs, rights and desires at all times. All work is carried out in an expedient & efficient manner to achieve the best possible outcome in the shortest time. Hypnotherapists are ethical, honest, friendly, fair, patient and understanding.

All our practitioners practice this code of ethics and standards with every client.
